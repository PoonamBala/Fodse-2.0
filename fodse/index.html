Who We Are

The Forum of Data Science Engineers (FODSE) is a highly premium club of SVCE founded on the 6th of December 2020. It was formed by the collaboration of IETE and AIT, SVCE. FODSE is devoted for the students who are passionate towards Data Science, Data Analytics, ML, AI and Statistics. We encourage our student members to build their skills and develop projects in the same given proper guidance from our forum mentors.

Our forum conducts webinars ,guest lectures ,technical events (such as hackathons , workshops , quizzes , case studies , etc.) frequently. Our goal is to turn data into information and information to insight.
